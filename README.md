# Confusense

Confusense is a live multi-topic feedback and voting platform designed to make training sessions smarter, interactive, and data-driven.

## What We Do

Confusense helps trainers and teams:

* Collect real-time feedback during sessions
* Enable live voting across multiple topics
* Identify confusion areas instantly
* Improve engagement and learning outcomes

## Our Vision

To transform passive training sessions into interactive, insight-driven experiences powered by real-time feedback.

## Focus Areas

* Live feedback systems
* Real-time voting mechanisms
* Scalable backend architecture
* Clean, intuitive product design
